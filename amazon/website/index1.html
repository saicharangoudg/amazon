About Me

I'm currently working as a Professor in the department of ECE at MREC, affiliated to JNTU,Hyderabad.. I have a total of 20 years teaching experience in academics including 8 years of Research experience. I completed B.Tech degree in Electronics and communication Engineering in the year 2005 and M.Tech in VLSI System Design from JNT University, Hyderabad in 2009. Received Ph.D from JNTU,Anantapur. I completed M.Sc (Psychology) in 2014 from DrBRAOU. I am enthusiastic to work in the field of VLSI. My area of interests includes Low power VLSI, Optimization of Analog and Mixed Signal Circuits in addition to digital system design using CADENCE tools. To my credit, I have 10 patents. and presented more than 70International/National Technical Papers in journals & conferences.
